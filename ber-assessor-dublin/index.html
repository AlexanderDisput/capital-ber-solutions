Our BER Services

Four dedicated services covering every stage of a property's energy rating journey.

🏡

Residential BER Assessment

A standard BER certificate for any home, required before a property is advertised for sale or rent. Learn more about what's involved on our BER Certificate page.

🌱

SEAI Grant Pre & Post-Works Assessment

Pre-works and post-works BER assessments required to support your SEAI retrofit grant application and confirm the energy improvements made.

Green Mortgage Audit

A BER assessment carried out to meet the energy rating requirements of green mortgage products offered by Irish lenders.

📐

Technical Advisor Survey

An independent technical survey and advisory report to guide cost-effective energy upgrades ahead of works or a BER reassessment.
