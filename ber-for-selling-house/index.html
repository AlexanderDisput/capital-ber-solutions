Why You Need a BER Before Going to Market

Irish legislation requires that a valid BER certificate is in place before a property is advertised for sale, and the BER rating must be displayed in property advertisements. Selling without one can delay your sale and create compliance issues for your estate agent.

⚖️

Legal Requirement

A valid BER certificate must be obtained and its rating displayed before your property can be legally advertised for sale.
